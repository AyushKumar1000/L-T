About Larsen & Toubro

Larsen & Toubro is a major Indian multinational conglomerate, with business interests in engineering, construction, manufacturing, technology, information technology, and financial services. It is one of the world's largest and most respected companies in the engineering and construction space.

The company is committed to building a better tomorrow through innovation, technology, and responsible business practices.

With a presence in over 50 countries, L&T continues to expand its global footprint, delivering landmark projects across various sectors including infrastructure, power, heavy engineering, and IT.
