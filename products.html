Featured Projects

Mumbai Metro

The Mumbai Metro project by L&T (Larsen & Toubro) is a major urban transit initiative aimed at reducing traffic congestion and improving public transport. L&T played a key role in the construction of Metro Line 3 and Line 4, involving advanced tunneling and elevated corridor engineering.

Hyderabad Metro Rail

The Hyderabad Metro Rail project, executed by L&T, is one of the world's largest Public-Private Partnership (PPP) metro projects. It spans over 69 km and integrates seamlessly with urban infrastructure to enhance city mobility.

Smart City Solutions

L&T's Smart City Solutions focus on developing digitally integrated, sustainable urban infrastructure. Their projects include smart surveillance, traffic management, energy-efficient utilities, and e-governance systems.

Chennai Metro Phase 2 - Corridor 5 (Madhavaram-Sholinganallur)

L&T, in collaboration with AEON, has installed India's longest precast U-girder (33.33 meters) at Ramapuram, marking a significant milestone in this metro expansion project.

Kacchi Dargah–Bidupur Bridge, Bihar

A joint venture between L&T and Daewoo E&C is constructing this 9.76 km bridge over the Ganges River, expected to be India's longest bridge upon completion in July 2025.

Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly Building

L&T has commenced construction of a new ₹617 crore assembly building in Amaravati, covering 11.21 lakh sq ft. The project, part of the state's capital development, is slated for completion within 18 months.
